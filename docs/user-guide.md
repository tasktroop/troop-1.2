# LeadOS User Guide

Welcome to LeadOS! This guide is designed to help local SME teams capture leads effortlessly and streamline multi-platform communications securely.

### Signing up and creating your org
Navigate to the Register page. Enter your details and define your Organizational Unit logically. By default, the creator becomes the 'Admin'. From your Settings panel, you can natively invite secondary 'Managers' or generic 'Agents'.

### Adding and managing leads
Click the blue "+ Add Lead" button within the Leads module. Complete inputs for phone variables and dynamic stages mapping exactly to generic endpoints. Your leads list will instantly refresh. 
- *Pro Tip:* Use the 3-dot menu to seamlessly shift Stages targeting pipeline velocity mappings directly internally.

### Using AI drafts
Don't know what to write? Click a lead, and select "Generate AI Draft". LeadOS detects the Lead Stage logically calling internal AI templates generating custom messages saving hours a month! Always review the output logically before approving.

### Sending WhatsApp messages
From any verified AI Draft, managers or agents with standard approvals can explicitly trigger internal SMS / WhatsApp workflows. Ensure leads haven't "OPTED-OUT" directly since LeadOS strictly blocks outgoing messaging to un-subscribed targets automatically natively.

### Scheduling social media posts
Navigate to Social Calendar natively selecting days. Add dynamic prompts, assign platforms, and let AI build social caption drafts internally spanning customized hashtags! Buffer APIs intercept your "Schedule" securely processing standard payloads implicitly in the background seamlessly.

### Understanding the approval workflow
Agents can generate AI templates, however, they must request "Validation". Managers and Admins access the "Approvals" dashboard monitoring flagged responses, approving them securely sending WhatsApp traces outright automatically seamlessly!

### Reading your analytics dashboard
Explore generic KPIs internally spanning 30d trailing views mapping exact funnel velocities, AI cost impacts scaling tokens securely internally! Conversion rates seamlessly auto-calculate mapped to Razorpay capture flows natively.
